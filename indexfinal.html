💖 In Every Giggle, In Every Tear 💖

~ A Complete Story of Us ~

💖 Chapter 1: When Strangers Found Home in a “Heyy” 💖

It started with something so small — a notification, a gentle ping. Not fate, not a miracle… just a “Heyy it’s me… Chatterbox.”

And there I was — a boy, unaware that this simple text would change everything.

“Hey, it’s me Nishant… aka Murthy,” I typed back, smiling without knowing why. “You can call me anything you like — just don't forget me.”

We didn’t know it yet, but that was the moment a new world began. A world where hearts were still learning to trust, but smiles had already found their way.

Chapter 2: The Giggles That Made Me Fall 💫

Her laugh wasn’t just a sound. It was therapy. It was magic. The kind that makes you forget everything wrong with the world.

When she laughed, it felt like spring bloomed in my chest. Like the world finally had music again.

At first, we’d text until our fingers hurt. But that wasn’t enough.

Calls started to replace silence. And those calls — they weren’t just conversations. They were memories in the making.

One day, she said, “You make me laugh like no one else does…”

And in that giggle, I found something sacred. Love? Maybe not yet. But I knew — I never wanted to stop hearing her laugh.

Chapter 3: From Laughter to Love❤️

We never planned it. We didn’t even see it coming.

But slowly, the “goodnights” became softer. The “are you okay?” came even when we were fine.

The comfort grew. The calls became confessions.

One night, under a quiet sky, we hesitated…

“Do you think this is… love?”

“I think it already was.”

And then we said it. “I love you.”

Not because we had to. But because our hearts were tired of holding it in.

That moment wasn’t loud — but my entire world paused to hear it.

Chapter 4: The Video Call That Changed It All❤️

Our first VC? Oh, it was adorable chaos. I was fixing my hair. She was hiding her blush behind a pillow.

We barely said anything — but we didn’t need to. Our eyes spoke volumes.

I was nervous. She looked like the softest dream.

We kept giggling, fumbling — but something about that call changed everything.

From pixels to real feelings. From voices to visions.

A month later, we had another video call — this time with less awkwardness, more connection.

That call didn’t just connect our devices — it connected two souls who were already in love.

Chapter 5: The Days That Weren’t Easy

We had our storms. Days when I was drowning in work. Days when she cried silently, wishing I had called.

She felt like I forgot her. I felt like I failed her.

But even in those quiet fights and aching silences, we never gave up.

We wrote poems instead of apologies. We used love as our language.

Like the time I said, “I don’t bleed but I feel your pain, Like stars that ache in midnight rain…”

Because even when we were hurting — we were still holding on.

Chapter 6: My Periods Hit Today… And I Was Ready❤️

I remember her message: “My periods hit today…”

I didn’t flinch. I didn’t panic.

I said: “I’m ready. Bring on the cravings, the cuddles, the chaos. I’m here, sweetheart.”

Because love isn’t just dates and roses — it’s being her safe place when she’s in pain.

She laughed through her cramps that day. Not because it didn’t hurt…

But because she had someone who stayed, without being asked.

Chapter 7: The Fight, The Tears, The Realization

We fought. I said something I didn’t mean. She cried. I cried harder.

That day — I saw her tears and broke down.

I thought, “Did I lose her?”

But she held my hand through the phone and said:

“Mistakes? They’ll happen. But leaving you? That’s never an option.”

That’s the day I realized — love doesn’t mean being perfect. It means staying — even when it hurts.

Chapter 8: The Girl Who Made Me Feel Like a Kid Again❤️

She scolds me when I’m lazy. Makes me study when I’m distracted.

She pampers me like a mom. Flirts with me like a lover.

And me? I become a kid around her. Spoiled. Safe. Soft.

She brings out the version of me I didn’t know existed — the silly, playful, carefree one who just wants her attention.

Every “study now!” or “go sleep” is her way of saying: “I love you, you idiot.”

And every time — I smile, and I fall a little more.

Chapter 9: The Story Beyond Screens❤️

We haven’t met. No dinner dates. No holding hands in a park.

But what we have? It’s real. Raw. Rare.

We turned video calls into hugs. Voice notes into lullabies.

And distance? It only made our bond stronger.

Even when we’re miles apart, I feel her beside me.

Because love doesn’t always need proximity — it just needs persistence.

“I’ll love you,” I said, “in every way I can. Through texts, through efforts, through storms. I’ll love you even when it’s hard.”

And I meant it.

💍 Chapter 10: Our Forever❤️

We’ve come so far — from “Heyy” to “I can’t live without you.”

We’ve faced fears, laughed through calls, cried through misunderstandings.

And today, I say it without doubt — “I choose you. Not just today, but every day.”

We aren’t perfect. But we’re real. And that’s enough.

Let the world call it virtual. Let them say it won’t last.

But we know the truth:

✨ This Is Us — A thousand poems, a million moments, and one forever. ❤️
